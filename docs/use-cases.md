# Use Cases – Where Athsany’s Decision Brain Fits

Athsany is designed as a decision brain layer for **high‑uncertainty, event‑driven environments**.  
Finance is our most complete live demonstration, but the same architecture extends into other domains.

---

## 1. Finance / Markets

**中文說明**  
在金融市場裡，Athsany 不只是發出單次交易訊號，而是把「事件 → 風險 → 策略 → 執行 → 檢討」整條鏈路長期記錄下來，變成可追溯、可學習的決策歷史。

**What Athsany sees**

- Live price and order‑book movements  
- Macro data releases, central bank events, company news  
- Cross‑asset signals (FX, indices, commodities, rates, derivatives)

**What the decision brain does**

- Builds an event → risk → strategy → execution → review loop on top of markets  
- Tracks how portfolios respond to shocks and trends over time  
- Records every decision, outcome, and adjustment into decision memory

**Why it matters**

- Turns fragmented trades and ideas into a traceable decision history  
- Helps teams see which patterns and reactions actually work over the long run

---

## 2. Banking / Supervision

**中文說明**  
在銀行與監管場景中，Athsany 更像一個「小型金管局／實時監察大腦」，長期記錄事件與監管反應，讓機構不再只是逐單個案處理，而是累積真正的監管與風控記憶。

**What Athsany sees**

- Internal risk metrics, limits, and exposures  
- Transaction and behavior signals  
- External events: regulation changes, macro stress, market disruptions

**What the decision brain does**

- Runs a continuous loop of event → risk → supervisory response → outcome → review  
- Builds a long‑term memory of which supervisory actions worked under which conditions  
- Supports “small central‑bank / real‑time supervision brain” style monitoring

**Why it matters**

- Moves from case‑by‑case reactions to a persistent supervision memory  
- Allows oversight teams to learn systematically from past crises and near‑misses

---

## 3. Enterprise / Supply Chain

**中文說明**  
在企業與供應鏈場景，Athsany 把原本靠人臨場救火的決策，變成可以重複、可回看、可優化的決策系統，幫你記住每次斷供、延誤背後的事件與選擇。

**What Athsany sees**

- Supplier status, logistics events, lead‑time changes  
- Inventory levels, demand signals, pricing shifts  
- External shocks: weather, geopolitics, regulation, infrastructure incidents

**What the decision brain does**

- Maps events into supply‑chain risk states (delay, shortage, concentration)  
- Suggests mitigation strategies (re‑routing, re‑allocation, hedging, contract changes)  
- Logs decisions and outcomes into decision memory for future disruptions

**Why it matters**

- Turns ad‑hoc firefighting into a repeatable decision infrastructure  
- Helps enterprises learn how their operations actually behave under stress

---

## 4. Public Risk / City Operations

**中文說明**  
在公共風險與城市營運場景，Athsany 將各種災害、事故、基建訊號整合起來，記錄政府／城市每次應對的決策與結果，逐步形成一個可學習的「城市決策大腦」。

**What Athsany sees**

- Environmental and infrastructure signals (weather, power, traffic, sensors)  
- Public safety and incident reports  
- Policy changes and city‑level interventions

**What the decision brain does**

- Runs a persistent loop: event → risk assessment → response strategy → execution → review  
- Captures how cities or agencies respond to crises and slow‑burn risks  
- Builds a cross‑event memory of what responses worked, where, and why

**Why it matters**

- Helps public institutions move from isolated incident reports to a long‑term risk memory  
- Supports more consistent, explainable, and improvable decisions at city scale

---

Athsany’s core does not change across these domains:  
it remains an always‑on decision brain with long‑term decision memory, sitting above events and data, and closing the loop from **event → risk → strategy → execution → review**.
